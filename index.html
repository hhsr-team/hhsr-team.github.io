About HHS ROBOTICS

The Hayward High Robotics Club is a collaborative, student-run robotics and engineering organization based at Hayward High School in Hayward, California.

The team participates and competes in the Pioneers in Engineering biannual robotics competition at the University of California, Berkeley.

Over the course of the school year, club members will learn a variety of essential computer, engineering, and robotics skills all while creating close bonds with a small group of motivated, like-minded peers. Anyone interested in STEM, especially those interested in technology and engineering are strongly encouraged to apply.
